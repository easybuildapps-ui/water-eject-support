Water Eject – Support

Last updated: December 10, 2025

Welcome to the support page for Water Eject. If you have questions about the app, need help with a feature, or want to report a problem, you can use the contact information below.

Contact

The fastest way to reach us is by email:

easybuildapps@gmail.com

What to Include in Your Message

To help us respond quickly, please include the following details (if possible):

  • Device model (for example, iPhone 15 Pro)
  • iOS version
  • App version
  • A short description of the issue
  • Screenshots or screen recordings (optional, but helpful)

Refunds and Purchases

All in-app purchases in Water Eject are processed through the Apple App Store. If you need a refund, please submit a request via your Apple ID account or the Report a Problem page provided by Apple. We do not process payments or refunds directly.

Feedback and Feature Requests

If you have ideas on how to improve Water Eject, feel free to send them to us. We read all feedback and use it to plan future updates and improvements.
