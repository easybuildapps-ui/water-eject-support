We do not ask you to create an account in the app and we do not request your name, email address or phone number inside the app.

1.1. Usage Data

Usage Data includes information about how you interact with the app, such as opened screens, taps, session duration, language, device type, operating system version, country or region, IP address, device identifiers and other technical details generated by your device.

If you make in-app purchases or activate a subscription, the app may process the following information:

We do not use the collected information to track you across apps and websites owned by other companies and we do not sell your data to third parties for advertising purposes.

Usage Data is generally retained for a limited period and used only for internal purposes described in this Policy. When data is no longer required, we take reasonable steps to delete or anonymize it.
